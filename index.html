NDVI Analysis Study

In this study, I calculated the Normalized Difference Vegetation Index (NDVI) for the Trivandrum region to understand vegetation health over time. Satellite imagery from Landsat-8/9 (2015 and 2025) was downloaded from USGS Earth Explorer dated 2015 and 2025. To streamline the workflow, model builder was used to automate the processing steps. The red (Band 4) and near-infrared (Band 5) layers were first clipped to the study area and NDVI was computed using the formula: NDVI = (B5 - B4)/ (B5 + B4) The results show that vegetation health has improved significantly over the last 10 years, possibly due to reduced human activity during the COVID-19 period or regional conservation policies put forth by the government.
