About Me

I am a recent BSc (Hons) graduate from the University of Plymouth, where I was awarded the prestigious International Excellence Scholarship throughout my undergraduate studies. I have a strong foundation in GIS and Remote Sensing and I am passionate about combining geospatial technology with AI and machine learning to support better disaster management.

My interests include weather and environmental prediction, risk mapping and developing data-driven solutions for real-world challenges. From a young age, I dreamed of being Wonder Woman and helping others; today that passion motivates me to use technology to improve community resilience and public safety.

I have developed solid technical experience as a GIS Developer with skills in digitizing, georeferencing, map design and cartography, raster processing, raster calculator, model builder and Python automation for geospatial workflows. I have also independently taught myself Python and machine learning techniques to deepen my analytical capabilities.

I am currently beginning winter training in Remote Sensing and AI-powered GIS at the Indian Space Academy, followed by an internship project there. This opportunity will allow me to strengthen my technical experience and apply what I’ve learned to real-world geospatial applications.

Beyond academics, I enjoy contributing to community service through volunteering. In my free time, I like photography, binge-watching Netflix, and traveling to explore new places and cultures.
