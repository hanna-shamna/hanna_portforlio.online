NDVI Analysis Study

In this study, I calculated the Normalized Difference Vegetation Index (NDVI) for the Trivandrum region to understand vegetation health over time. Satellite imagery from Landsat-8/9 (2015 and 2025) was downloaded from USGS Earth Explorer. To streamline the workflow, model builder was used to automate the processing steps. The red (Band 4) and near-infrared (Band 5) layers were first clipped to the study area and NDVI was computed using the formula: NDVI = (B5 ‚àí B4) / (B5 + B4) Results show significant improvement in vegetation over 10 years.
